<p><strong>The Intelligent Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Positive Displacement Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intelligent pump market encompasses two main types: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move fluids, making them ideal for high flow rates and low-viscosity applications. In contrast, positive displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed amount of fluid and forcing it through the discharge, suitable for high-viscosity fluids and precise flow control. Both types are increasingly integrated with smart technology, enabling real-time monitoring and optimization for enhanced efficiency and performance across various industries.</p></p>
