<p><p>Inverted sugar syrups are a mixture of glucose and fructose, produced by hydrolyzing sucrose. These syrups are prized for their sweetness, stability, and ability to enhance moisture retention in food products. Their lower crystallization tendency compared to regular sugar makes them ideal for various applications, including confectionery, bakery items, beverages, and sauces.</p><p>The inverted sugar syrups market is experiencing significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 7.00% during the forecast period.
