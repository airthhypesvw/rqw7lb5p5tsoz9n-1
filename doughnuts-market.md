<p><strong>The Doughnuts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cake Style</li><li>Yeast Style</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The doughnut market can be divided into two main types: cake style and yeast style. Cake-style doughnuts are denser, made from a cake batter that results in a moist, crumbly texture, often flavored and glazed. Conversely, yeast-style doughnuts are lighter and fluffier, made from a yeast-leavened dough that rises during proofing. They are typically airy and can be filled with creams or jellies. Each style appeals to different consumer preferences, driving diverse offerings in the market.</p></p>
