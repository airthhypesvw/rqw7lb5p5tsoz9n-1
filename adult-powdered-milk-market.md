<p><p>The adult powdered milk market consists of two primary types: whole milk powder and skim milk powder. Whole milk powder contains all the fat and nutrients found in fresh milk, making it rich and creamy, ideal for those seeking a full flavor and higher calorie content. In contrast, skim milk powder has had most of the fat removed, offering a lower calorie option while retaining essential proteins and vitamins, catering to health-conscious consumers looking for nutritional benefits without excess fat.</p></p>
